# Academic Manuscript Revision

## When to Use
- First draft is complete and needs polishing
- Manuscript requires objective review
- Improving flow, rhythm, and clarity
- Seeking external perspective on written work

## When NOT to use

- 不适用于论文初稿撰写阶段（需先有完整草稿）
- 当用户没有提供足够信息来判断适用性时，先询问而非假设
- 如果已有更专门的 skill 覆盖当前场景，优先使用那个 skill
- 不要在时间极度紧迫时跳过 skill 的核心流程——宁可缩短范围也不要跳过必要步骤

## Prerequisites
- Complete first draft finished
- Time available for revision process

## Revision Methodologies

### Method 1: Cooling Method (冷却法)

**Procedure:**
1. Set aside the draft for a period (days or months) after completion
2. Allow emotional detachment from the work
3. Return with fresh perspective

**Purpose:**
- Overcome author bias and attachment
- Enable objective identification of flaws
- "The onlooker sees clearly" - gain distance from original emotions/thoughts

**Benefits:**
- Flaws become visible after time passes
- Allows reflection on intent, structure, and language
- Reveals issues invisible during initial writing

### Method 2: Reading Aloud Method (诵读法)

**Procedure:**
1. Read the draft aloud several times
2. Listen for rhythm, flow, and connection issues
3. Mark points that feel "sticking" (沾唇) or "noisy" (拗噪)

**Purpose:**
- Detect issues in flow and connection
- Identify missing words or awkward phrasing
- Catch emotional tone mismatches

**Mechanism:**
- Chinese characters have tones and rhythm
- Problematic passages create audible friction
- Rhythm issues indicate structural problems

**Example:**
- Adding connecting words (如 "而") can slow rhythm and add depth
- "仕宦而至将相" demonstrates rhythm enhancement

### Method 3: Seeking Advice Method (求教法)

**Procedure:**
1. Ask teachers, classmates, or friends to review
2. Request specific critique on content and structure
3. Collect and evaluate feedback objectively

**Purpose:**
- Overcome self-bias ("The player is confused")
- Gain external perspective ("The onlooker sees clearly")
- Receive fair evaluation of strengths and weaknesses

**Benefits:**
- Peers provide objective assessment
- Help identify unnecessary content (繁简当否)
- Reveal blind spots in reasoning or structure

## Variables
- `cooling_period`: Duration to set aside before review (recommended: days to months)
- `reviewer_feedback`: External critique received from peers or mentors

## Recommended Workflow

1. **Immediately after drafting**: Apply Cooling Method first
2. **After cooling period**: Use Reading Aloud Method
3. **After self-revision**: Apply Seeking Advice Method
4. **Final stage**: Integrate feedback and polish

## Output
A polished, refined manuscript ready for submission.

## Gotchas

- **不要在写完当天就改**：冷却法要求间隔数天到数月，当天改等于没改——大脑还在"完成"模式，看不进问题
- **不要用诵读法跳过中文特有的音韵问题**：普通话四声会掩盖节奏问题，用粤语/方言读反而更容易发现拗口
- **不要把"求教"变成"求认同"**：找老师同学看的时候，要请他们提意见而非"你觉得行不行"——后者会引导对方说好话
- **不要同时用多种方法改**：冷却 → 诵读 → 求教 → 整合，这四个阶段不要压缩到一天完成
- **不要忽略"繁简当否"判断**：精简掉的内容可能恰好是核心论点，删之前问自己"这句话在论证什么"

## Risk Blacklist

- **未冷却就提交修改稿**：这是最常见的低级错误，评审人会立即发现
- **在求教阶段泄露匿名审稿人身份**：如果参与的是双盲评审，告知他人审稿人身份是违规的
- **修改论文时引入新错误**：每轮修改后必须做全文一致性检查，不能只改提到的地方
